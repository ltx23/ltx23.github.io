Prompt Tips

Be explicit about motion

Instead of writing a broad cinematic phrase, describe camera movement, subject action, pacing, and visual focus more clearly.

A shot-by-shot structure often makes prompts easier to improve because each part of the scene has a clearer job.

A small first pass helps you validate the direction before you spend more time refining the workflow.
